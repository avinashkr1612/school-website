Facalities and Infrastructre

school's infrastructre and various facalities being provided by the Alok Public School, Bremo are discussed below.

Location & Approach

Alok Public Scchool is located in Urban area of Jharkhand state of India. In Makoli area of Bermo block of Bokaro district. School is approchable by all weather roads.

Library

School owns a library for students and staff and there are near about 3000 books in the library

Computer Lab

There is a computer lab in school for learning purpose and almost 20 computers are there in the computer lab.

Play Ground

School also owns a play ground for sports and extracurricular activities.

Computer Aided Learning (CAL)

To make teaching learning process joyful, interesting and easy to understand through audio-visual aids School has implemented CAL (Computer Aided Learning) or better known as Computer Based Learning. CAL is currently functional in the school.
